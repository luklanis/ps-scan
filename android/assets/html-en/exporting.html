To export payments as a DTA File ESR Scanner needs your IBAN and at least two lines of your address. Please enter this information in ESR Scanner's settings.

You can find the export execution over History and then DTA .

The file name on the bottom of the payment details points to the file that includes that payment.

Each payment will be exported once. If you want to re-export, please select the payment and press in the right of the file name.
